StarBurst Idle Data Deletion

We don’t store any of your personal data ourselves. All profiles, purchase history, achievements, and device identifiers are managed by Google Play Services and the Google Mobile Ads SDK.

Revoke Access

To remove our app’s access to your Google account data, go to your Google Account settings:

  1. Visit myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Find “StarBurst Idle” in the list of connected apps
  3. Click “Remove Access”
