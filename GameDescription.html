StarBurst Idle — Game Description

StarBurst Idle is a space-themed idle incremental where you grow a star by generating and directing hydrogen toward cosmic milestones. It’s an active idle experience—most progress happens while the game is open—backed by deep prestige layers, new currencies, and upgrade trees that keep the strategy evolving.

Developer: Bolster Entertainment • Category: Simulation
Contains ads • In-app purchases • Cloud saves

How to Play

  • Generate hydrogen automatically and spend it to increase your star’s mass.
  • Use upgrades and click boosts to accelerate growth.
  • Prestige through physics-themed layers to unlock new currencies and meta-progression.

Prestige Layers

  • Core Fusion: Sacrifice built-up multipliers for powerful upgrades.
  • StarBurst: Shatter your star to earn StarBurst currency and unlock bigger boosts.
  • Black Hole & Universe Resets: Collapse into a black hole, then reset the universe for fresh twists and long-term meta-upgrades.

Key Features

  • Exponential growth: Watch your star’s mass skyrocket with each upgrade.
  • No long offline timers: Designed for continuous, active directing gameplay.
  • Auto-play + active clicking: Automate hydrogen or click for faster progress.
  • Layered upgrade trees: Plan your route through each prestige tier.
  • Tutorials & achievements: Learn quickly and strive for 100% completion.
  • Fair monetization: Earn 2×–3× speed boosts via achievements, ads, or optional IAP—fully playable without spending.
  • Cloud saves: Pick up where you left off across devices.
