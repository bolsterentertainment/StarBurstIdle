StarBurst Idle Data Deletion (iOS)

Last updated: 15 Sep 2025

We don’t store any of your personal data ourselves. On iOS, all profiles, purchase history, achievements, and device identifiers are managed by Apple Game Center, Apple In‑App Purchase (StoreKit), Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), Unity Services (Services Core, IAP, Authentication with Game Center, and Unity Cloud Save), and our marketing attribution provider Tenjin.

Uninstall to Stop Collection

Uninstalling the app stops all further data collection from your device.

Sign Out (Game Center)

If you wish to stop submitting scores/achievements and using Game Center sign‑in:

  • Open Settings → Game Center and tap Sign Out.

Signing out prevents new leaderboard/achievement submissions and Game Center‑based sign‑in. Game Center data is managed by Apple; to manage or request deletion of your Game Center data, please consult Apple’s Privacy Policy or visit privacy.apple.com.

Delete Cloud Save (Unity Cloud Save)

Your cloud save is stored anonymously and linked to your Game Center identity. You can delete it at any time by performing a Hard Reset inside the game. This will erase your local save and remove the associated Unity Cloud Save.

Ad Personalization & Tracking (AdMob)

You can limit or disable tracking used for personalized ads on iOS:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and turn off Allow Apps to Request to Track, or deny tracking for StarBurst Idle.
  • (Optional) In Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising, you can turn off Personalized Ads for Apple’s own ad platform.

Reset or Limit Your Advertising ID (IDFA)

When tracking is denied, the IDFA is not made available to apps. On some iOS versions you may also see a Reset Advertising Identifier option under Privacy settings.

Tenjin (Marketing Attribution)

We use Tenjin to attribute installs and measure campaign performance. Tenjin processes device identifiers (e.g., IDFA when permitted), IP address, SKAdNetwork postbacks, and app open/session events for this purpose only. For details and contact options, see the Tenjin Privacy Policy.
